## Core Theories

### CCI Protocol: Chaos → Cognition → Intelligence
Information flows through three stages: raw data → pattern recognition → sovereign action.

### The Four Stages: Dam → Spek → Vā → Bū/Di
- **दम्** (Dam): Raw potential/ground truth
- **स्पेक्** (Spek): Validation/collective verification
- **वा** (Vā): Systemic flow/structure
- **बु** (Bū): Material substrate/texture
- **दि** (Di): Sovereignty/clarity

**Insight:** Modern systems are Bū-हीन (textureless)—they optimize flow and polish at the cost of grounding.

### Connectivity Theory
Prefixes वा/बु/दि classify domains: systemic, material, and sovereign.

## Neological Registry

### Vigilantes: सतर्ज (Satarja)

**Etymology:** स (togetherness) + तर्क (crossing/logic) + ज (born from)

**Definition:** One born from collective act of overcoming fear and evil.

**Shloka:** "यैर्भयं तरितं लोके, पापं विदारितं तमः।
तैः सतर्जाः समुत्पन्नाः, रक्षकास्तमसः परे ।।"
> Those by whom fear is crossed in the world, evil and darkness are pierced—from them the Satarjas are born, protectors beyond darkness.

**Expansion N Declension:**
- सतर्जाः (m. plural) | सतर्जानाः (f. plural) | सतर्जानौ (dual pair) | सतर्जात् (mixed)

---

### Nerd Taxonomy: नारदम् (Nāradam)

**Definition:** Knowledge-seeker characterized by restlessness settled only through information acquisition.

**Four Classifications:**
- **बु-नारदम्:** Material Nerd (ecology, tangible reality)
- **वा-नारदम्:** Systemic Nerd (logic, programming, automation)
- **दि-नारदम्:** Transcendent Nerd (theology, mysticism, sovereignty)
- **नारदाक्षरम्:** Word Nerd (morphology, etymology, linguistic architecture)
