## Neological Registry

### Vigilantes: सतर्ज (Satarja)

**Sanskrit:** सतर्ज (Satarja)

**Etymology:** 
- स (sa-) = togetherness
- तर्क (tarka) / तॄ (tṝ) = reasoning / to cross/overcome
- ज (-ja) = born from

**Definition:** One born from the shared, collective act of overcoming fear and evil. A protector who traverses darkness, penetrates fear, and breaks the power of evil through community-grounded sovereign action.

**Canonical Shloka:**
```
ॐ यैर्भयं तरितं लोके, पापं विदारितं तमः।
तै: सतर्जा: समुत्पन्ना:, रक्षकास्तमसः परे।।

"Those by whom fear is crossed in the world, evil and darkness are pierced—
from them the Satarjas are born, protectors beyond darkness."
```

**Expansion N Declension:**
- **सतर्जः** (m. singular) = A male vigilante
- **सतर्जा** (f. singular) = A female vigilante
- **सतर्जाः** (m. plural) = Male vigilantes
- **सतर्जानाः** (f. plural) = Female vigilantes
- **सतर्जानौ** (dual) = Unified male-female pair of vigilantes
- **सतर्जात्** (mixed collective) = Mixed group of vigilantes

**Register Applications:**
- **Poetic:** सतर्जानौ कार्यं करतः—न द्वे, किन्तु एकम्। ("The [male and female] vigilantes work together—not two, but one.")
- **Administrative:** सतर्जा: आज्ञा... ("Directive from the vigilantes...")
- **Scholarly:** For documenting vigilante systems, organizational structure, social movements

### Nerd Taxonomy: नारदम् (Nāradam)

**Sanskrit:** नारदम् (Nāradam)

**Etymology:**
- न (na) + राद् (rād-) = "The Restless One"
- Archetype: Nārada, the divine sage who crosses boundaries and carries knowledge across realms
- Phonetic resonance with English "Nerd"

**Universal Definition:** A knowledge-seeker characterized by restlessness that is only settled through information acquisition and intellectual mastery.

---

#### The Four Nerd Classifications (Connectivity Theory)

Each classification uses the prefixes from Connectivity Theory to describe different "domains of Restless Devotion":

| Sanskrit | Classification | Description |
|----------|---|---|
| **बु-नारदम्** (Bū-nārada) | **Material Nerd** | Obsessed with soil, ecology, tangible texture. Knowledge resides in sensory friction and material reality. |
| **वा-नारदम्** (Vā-nārada) | **Systemic Nerd** | Obsessed with logic, programming, automation, orchestrated elegance. Masters of flow and structure. |
| **दि-नारदम्** (Di-nārada) | **Transcendent Nerd** | Obsessed with theology, mysticism, high-vibration ideals. Seekers of sovereign clarity and meaning. |
| **नारदाक्षरम्** (Nāradākṣara) | **Word Nerd** | Obsessed with morphology, etymology, linguistic architecture. The akṣara (imperishable) nature of language work. |

**Meta-Property:** नारदाक्षरम् functions as a "Self-Insult Wrapped in Honor"—acknowledges social perception of root-obsession while affirming the sacred, imperishable nature of linguistic work.
